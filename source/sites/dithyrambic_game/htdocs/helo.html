Helo is a 2D, virtual world where you and a friend can ...

  • gather gems
  • recruit soldiers
  • build your PMC

Build

Build your base by mining resources and spending resources on sructures, upgrades, or recruits. Build before night falls and hostile PMCs converge on your base.

Defend

Defend your remote mining operations from agitators. Hostile PMCs will use the cover of night to seige your base - defend your base, or risk mission failure.

Conquer

Conquer hostile mining outposts to weaken other PMCs, then strike them down for good. Control all mining outposts and destroy all hostile PMCs to win.

The DNA of Helo

Helo was born from our love of co-op helicopter combat. As our first commercial project together, we followed the Risk of Rain approach: build a tight 2D experience first, with plans to eventually expand into 3D. We merged this aerial action with Kingdom-style base building, but instead of playing a passive monarch, you provide devastating air support. Inspired by MGSV, we set our game in Central Africa to reflect the gritty reality of PMC-dominated resource conflicts.

Roadmap

Helo is currently in a playable state with about 5 minutes of gameplay. We are in the process of refining gameplay elements like pacing, AI behavior, and asset implementation. Playtesters are vitally important in this stage of development - send us an email if you would like to playtest.

The Team

The team consists of two developers, Julian and Josh. We met over a decade ago via Dust 514 - where we first got a taste for co-op helicopter-based gameplay. We live on opposite sides of the US, but have remained good friends.
